Venue

DSAA 2024 will take place at Bahia Resort Hotel in San Diego, CA, United States.

Tucked away on a fourteen-acre peninsula in Mission Bay, the resort is your beachside escape for experiencing the best of San Diego.

How to get there?

From the Airport

The Bahia Resort Hotel is approximately 10 miles from the San Diego International Airport (SAN) at Lindbergh Field. An average journey by taxi from the SAN airport to the conference venue takes 15 minutes and costs 25$ depending on traffic.
